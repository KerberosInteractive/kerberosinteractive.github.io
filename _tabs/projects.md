[Prudentia](https://github.com/KerberosInteractive/Prudentia){:target="_blank"} (_Private_)  
**A framework for sustainable, ethical, and intelligent software development**  
Prudentia is a comprehensive framework for maintaining software quality and consistency across human and AI collaboration. Whether you're working solo, with a team, or leveraging AI assistants, Prudentia ensures your project follows industry-proven standards while remaining sustainable and ethical.

[KINEM](https://github.com/KerberosInteractive/KINEM){:target="_blank"}  
**Kerberos Interactive New Entry Manager**  
A Windows utility to fully manage the "New" context submenu. Easily add, edit, or remove entries to customize your workflow and remove clutter.

[KIDR](https://github.com/KerberosInteractive/KIDR){:target="_blank"}  
**Kerberos Interactive Data Renamer**  
A lightweight, high-performance Blender add-on that batch renames the data-blocks of selected objects to match their respective object names. It's designed to be extremely fast and stable, handling thousands of objects in an instant without freezing Blender.
